# NFT Image Prompts — Category A: Character Equipment (60 Items)

## Style Guide (Apply to ALL prompts below)

**Base style prefix** (prepend to every prompt):
```
Dark fantasy RPG item icon, centered on pure black background (#000000), no text, no watermark, dramatic lighting with magical glow, highly detailed digital painting, dark medieval aesthetic with arcane energy, inspired by Diablo and Dark Souls item art
```

**Resolution**: Generate at 512×512. Downscale to 64×64 for icons.

**Color coding by rarity** (add to prompt):
- Common (Gray): `muted gray tones, simple materials, worn and humble`
- Uncommon (Green): `subtle green magical aura, polished materials`
- Rare (Blue): `blue arcane energy, enchanted glow, fine craftsmanship`
- Epic (Purple): `purple ethereal flames, ornate gold filigree, mystical runes`
- Legendary (Orange): `blazing orange-gold radiance, divine craftsmanship, ancient power`
- Mythic (Red/Gold): `red and gold cosmic energy, reality-warping visual distortion, godlike artifact`
