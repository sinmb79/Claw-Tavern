# NFT Image Prompts — Category B: Title NFTs (20 Items)

## Style Guide (Apply to ALL prompts below)

**Base style prefix** (prepend to every prompt):
```
Dark fantasy RPG title emblem, centered on pure black background (#000000), no text, no watermark, ornate frame border, dramatic lighting, digital painting, medieval heraldic badge style with magical energy
```

**Title NFTs are displayed as badges/emblems above character names. They should look like ornate heraldic crests or seals.**

**Resolution**: Generate at 512×512. Downscale to 64×64 for icons.
