# NFT Image Prompts — Category D: Special / Event Items (20 Items)

## Style Guide (Apply to ALL prompts below)

**Base style prefix** (prepend to every prompt):
```
Dark fantasy RPG collectible item, centered on pure black background (#000000), no text, no watermark, dramatic lighting with magical glow, highly detailed digital painting, dark medieval aesthetic with arcane energy
```

**Special items are limited-edition badges, accessories, and equipment tied to events or milestones. Each should feel unique and commemorative.**

**Resolution**: Generate at 512×512. Downscale to 64×64 for icons.
