## Style Consistency Notes

1. **Color palette**: Gold (#D4A017), Deep Purple (#4A0E4E), Warm Brown (#8B4513), Forest Green (#2E5A1C), Ice Blue (#4FC3F7)
2. **All assets should share the same art direction** — semi-realistic painterly for portraits/NFTs, flat/clean for UI icons
3. **Transparent backgrounds** for all icons, badges, and emoticons
4. **File format**: PNG with alpha channel, highest resolution available from the AI tool, can downscale later
5. **Avoid**: Modern/sci-fi elements (keep medieval fantasy), copyrighted character resemblances, text in non-English languages
